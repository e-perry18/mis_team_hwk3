Why Sooner Success Matters to Students

Survey responses from OU students show that most struggle to stay organized across multiple job websites and would use a centralized tool like Sooner Success.

1. Organization Across Many Platforms

Instead of tracking roles in scattered spreadsheets, notes apps, and browser tabs, students see all applications and materials in one hub.

Scenario: A junior applying to 15 internships can quickly check which companies have received tailored resumes and which need follow-ups.

2. Better Preparation and Confidence

Embedded resources and assessments help students practice interviews and strengthen weak areas before meeting recruiters.

Scenario: A sophomore nervous about case interviews can work through practice questions and track progress over time.

3. Personalized Career Planning

A personal overview page encourages reflection on strengths, weaknesses, and goals, guiding students toward better-fit opportunities.

Scenario: A freshman exploring majors can align their emerging interests with internships that build relevant skills.

4. Accessibility for All Class Years

The tool is useful for freshmen and sophomores seeking internships as well as juniors and seniors targeting full-time roles.

Scenario: A senior can track full-time applications while a first-year uses the same hub to prepare for their first campus job fair.

In the survey, about two-thirds of respondents said they would use a platform like Sooner Success, highlighting clear demand for this type of campus solution.
