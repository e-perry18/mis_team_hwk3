Project Timeline & Milestones

This timeline outlines how the Sooner Success team will move from an initial idea and survey results to a functional prototype for OU students.

Week 1–2: Discovery & Refinement

Finalize the problem statement, review survey findings, and prioritize the most valuable features for the initial version.

Week 3–4: Wireframes & UX

Design low-fidelity wireframes for the resume editor, resources page, overview dashboard, and assessment module using student feedback.

Week 5–7: Development Sprint

Build the core pages, implement data structures for saving applications and documents, and test responsive layouts.

Week 8–9: Testing & Feedback

Conduct usability tests with classmates, refine content, and adjust navigation or features based on student input.

Week 10: Final Demo & Reflection

Prepare a demo showcasing how Sooner Success supports students across class years and document lessons learned for future iterations.
