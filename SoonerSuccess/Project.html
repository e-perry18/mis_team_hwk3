Project Idea: Sooner Success Career Hub

Sooner Success is a centralized web hub that helps OU students organize their entire job and internship search in one place instead of juggling multiple disconnected platforms.

Problem for Campus Students

Students often switch between sites like LinkedIn, Handshake, and Indeed while also tracking resumes, cover letters, and interview prep, which makes it hard to stay organized and prepared.

Core Features

Personalized Resume & Cover Letter

Students store a base resume and cover letter, then quickly customize versions for each role while keeping everything organized by application.

Career Resources Hub

A curated page with interview tips, case study guides, and sample questions so students do not have to search across multiple websites.

Personal Overview & Strengths

A dashboard where students reflect on strengths, weaknesses, and career interests, helping them align applications with their profile.

Assessment & Skill Practice

Short assessments to practice critical thinking, problem solving, and data analysis to build interview-ready confidence.

Future Integrations

Long term, the platform could pull status updates from LinkedIn, Indeed, Handshake, and other tools so students can see completed and in-progress applications in one dashboard.
